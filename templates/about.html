About Us

We are students from Xavier Institute of Engineering, we have tried
to bring together Indian Classical Music and Artificial Intelligence.
Anyone can learn Indian Classical Music with the help of our application.
We have provided a virtual harmonium keyboard along with
free lessons, audios and videos for better understanding.
Our Goal is to create a one stop repository for Indian Classical Music.


We sincerely thank all the creators of the audio and video lessons
which were used in this application. We do not claim any rights
over the audio and video lessons.They were solely used for
learning purpose.
